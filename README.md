Userscript to download all pay statments from ADP
=====

Download all ADP pay statements as PDFs.

## How to use:

1. Open the [Pay Statements page](https://mypay-hub.idp.amazon-corp.com/) 
2. Click the Pay pill at the top
3. Click the Tampermonkey icon in your browser's toolbar, it may be hidden behind the puzzle piece.
4. Click "Download All Pay Statements" 
5. You may need to allow the site to download multiple files
